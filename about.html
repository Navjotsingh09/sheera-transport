About Seehra Transport

Your trusted logistics partner

Who We Are

Seehra Transport is a leading logistics and delivery company specializing in multi-drop and last-mile delivery services across the UK. With over a decade of experience, we've built our reputation on reliability, efficiency, and exceptional customer service.

Our team of professional drivers and logistics experts work tirelessly to ensure your deliveries reach their destination on time, every time. We understand that in today's fast-paced business environment, timely and reliable delivery isn't just a service—it's a necessity.

From small packages to large shipments, from single deliveries to complex multi-drop routes, we have the expertise and infrastructure to handle all your logistics needs with precision and care.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

01

Reliability

We understand that businesses depend on us. Our commitment to on-time delivery isn't just a promise—it's our standard operating procedure. We track every delivery and maintain a 98% on-time delivery rate.

02

Efficiency

Through advanced route optimization and logistics planning, we minimize delivery times and costs. Our efficient operations benefit both our clients and the environment.

03

Professionalism

Every member of our team represents your business during delivery. We maintain the highest standards of professionalism in appearance, conduct, and customer interaction.

04

Transparency

Clear communication is key to great service. We provide real-time tracking, proactive updates, and honest communication about any challenges that arise.

05

Flexibility

Every business has unique needs. We offer flexible delivery solutions that adapt to your schedule, your customers, and your business requirements.

06

Safety

The safety of our drivers, your goods, and the public is paramount. We maintain rigorous safety standards and ensure all vehicles are regularly serviced and inspected.
