Why Choose Seehra Transport?

As one of the UK's leading multi-drop and last-mile delivery specialists, Seehra Transport provides comprehensive courier and logistics solutions tailored to businesses of all sizes. Our professional delivery network covers every postcode in England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland, ensuring your parcels reach their destination on time, every time.

Whether you need same-day delivery in London, next-day express service to Edinburgh, or multi-drop distribution across Manchester, Birmingham, Leeds, Glasgow, or Liverpool, our experienced team and modern fleet guarantee reliable, tracked, and insured deliveries you can depend on.

Comprehensive Delivery Services

  • Multi-Drop Delivery: Efficient multi-stop routes for bulk deliveries to multiple locations across the UK
  • Last-Mile Logistics: Final delivery stage from depot to doorstep with real-time GPS tracking
  • Same-Day Courier: Urgent delivery within 4-6 hours for time-critical shipments
  • Next-Day Express: Reliable next business day delivery with morning, afternoon, or evening time slots
  • Standard Delivery: Cost-effective 2-3 day service for non-urgent parcels
  • B2B Logistics: Dedicated business solutions with account management and API integration

Service Coverage Areas

Our nationwide delivery network provides comprehensive coverage across all UK regions:

  • Major Cities: London, Birmingham, Manchester, Leeds, Glasgow, Edinburgh, Liverpool, Bristol, Sheffield, Newcastle
  • Regional Coverage: Midlands, North West, North East, Yorkshire, South West, South East, East Anglia, Wales, Scotland, Northern Ireland
  • Remote Areas: Scottish Highlands, Channel Islands, Isle of Man, Isle of Wight, Isles of Scilly
  • Special Access: Business parks, industrial estates, shopping centers, residential complexes

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We provide nationwide coverage across the entire United Kingdom including England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. We deliver to all postcodes including remote areas, Scottish Highlands, and islands with appropriate surcharges.

How quickly can you deliver?

We offer same-day delivery (collection and delivery within 4-6 hours), next-day express (before 12pm, 5pm, or 9pm), overnight service, and standard 2-3 day delivery. Timings vary by location and service selected.

How much does delivery cost?

Prices start from £4.99 for standard delivery. Exact pricing depends on parcel weight, dimensions, collection/delivery postcodes, and service speed. Use our instant quote calculator for accurate pricing. Business customers receive volume discounts.

Can I track my parcel?

Yes! All deliveries include real-time GPS tracking. You'll receive a unique tracking number via email/SMS. Track your parcel on our website or mobile app with live updates every step of the way from collection to delivery.

Are parcels insured?

All parcels include free insurance up to £100. You can purchase additional coverage up to £5,000 at booking. We offer comprehensive claims process with fast compensation for loss or damage (subject to terms and conditions).

Do you offer business accounts?

Yes, we provide dedicated B2B solutions including account management, API integration, volume discounts, 30-day invoice terms, and customized SLAs. Contact our business team for a tailored quote.
